# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We are committed to ensuring the security of DgenBooks. Below are the versions of our software that are currently being supported with security updates.

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 1.0.x   | :white_check_mark: |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The DgenBooks team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a vulnerability, please email us at `security@example.com` with a detailed description of the issue, the steps to reproduce it, and any potential impact. Please do not create a public GitHub issue.

We will respond to your report within 48 hours and will keep you informed of the progress of our investigation and any remediation efforts. We will publicly credit you for your discovery if you wish.

Thank you for helping keep DgenBooks and our users safe.
